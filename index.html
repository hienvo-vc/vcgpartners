Investing in Vietnam

With Asia’s fastest urbanization rate, Vietnam is home to nearly 100 million people – half below the age of 35 - and a fast-growing middle class eager to improve their lives. These factors contribute to a robust domestic consumption story that helps propel economic growth that consistently ranks as one of the world’s fastest. Vietnam has quickly become a manufacturing hub in the region with many of the world’s leading manufacturers are moving their production to Vietnam, benefitting from competitive wages, a largely educated workforce, and a stable government that has signed the most Free Trade Agreements in Southeast Asia

OUR SERVICES

Separately Managed Accounts (SMA) are the preferred vehicle of high-net-worth individuals and institutions as it offers customization in investment strategy, approach and management style. In our case, the customization lies in our pursuit of Alpha.


The core investment objective of our SMA service is to deliver unconstrained absolute return with concentrated long-term equities portfolio comprising of positions in superior businesses globally. Our strategy focuses on capturing highly idiosyncratic bottom-up fundamental returns in mispriced change, typically due to technology disruption, market misperception or corporate governance issues, with a goal of generating +50% return over 3-year period. Returns comprised of mostly long-term gains with minimal portfolio churn.


Our SMA business is spearheaded by a portfolio manager with 14 years of investment experience across developed and emerging markets coupled with multi-disciplinary experience across long-short equities, distressed debt, special situations and global small cap investing. The portfolio manager has managed global concentrated strategy at BM&O with 2 years of outperformance track records – Strategy Alpha of 28% in 2014 and +9% in 2015 relative to benchmark.

VCC: The Game Changer

The Singapore Variable Capital Company (VCC) legislation represents the city-state’s latest investment fund innovation. Adding to the existing suite of legal entities used as investment vehicle, the VCC can be used for all types of investment strategies. Some of the pivotal incentives of the structure include: Grant scheme by MAS to co-fund 70% of the qualifying fund setup expenses up to S$150,000 as well as Tax Incentives scheme under sections 13R (referred to as “Singapore Resident Fund Scheme”) and 13X (“Enhanced-Tier Fund Scheme”) of the Singapore Income Tax Act.
