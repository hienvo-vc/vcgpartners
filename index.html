Investing in Vietnam

With Asia’s fastest urbanization rate, Vietnam is home to nearly 100 million people – half below the age of 35 - and a fast-growing middle class eager to improve their lives. These factors contribute to a robust domestic consumption story that helps propel economic growth that consistently ranks as one of the world’s fastest. Vietnam has quickly become a manufacturing hub in the region with many of the world’s leading manufacturers are moving their production to Vietnam, benefitting from competitive wages, a largely educated workforce, and a stable government that has signed the most Free Trade Agreements in Southeast Asia.

VCC: The Game Changer

The Singapore Variable Capital Company (VCC) legislation represents the city-state’s latest investment fund innovation. Adding to the existing suite of legal entities used as investment vehicle, the VCC can be used for all types of investment strategies. Some of the pivotal incentives of the structure include: Grant scheme by MAS to co-fund 70% of the qualifying fund setup expenses up to S$150,000 as well as Tax Incentives scheme under sections 13R (referred to as “Singapore Resident Fund Scheme”) and 13X (“Enhanced-Tier Fund Scheme”) of the Singapore Income Tax Act. Combining the vibrant growth-oriented environment in Vietnam and the rest of Southeast Asia region; as well as key elements that elevate Singapore’s value proposition as a competitive asset management hub in the region, we offer various investment solutions to our exclusive Accredited Investor and Institutional Partners.
