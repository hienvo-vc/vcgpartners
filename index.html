Investing in Vietnam

Home to nearly 100 million people – half below the age of 35 – Vietnam has one of the fastest growing economies in the world. Rapid urbanization – the fastest in Asia – and an expanding middle class eager to improve their lives are contributing to a robust domestic consumption story that helps propel economic growth. Vietnam is quickly becoming a manufacturing hub in the region, with many of the world’s leading manufacturers moving their production to the country, benefitting from competitive wages, an educated workforce, and a stable government that has signed one the greatest number of free trade agreements in Southeast Asia.


Singapore as the Gateway to Southeast Asia

Singapore has long served as a gateway for investing in Southeast Asia thanks to its independent and efficient judiciary, a conducive tax system, and business-friendly laws and regulations. Today, the city-state is one of the world’s leading financial centers, with a stable government that continues to create a welcoming environment for fund managers and other global businesses.

OUR PRODUCTS

We manages various Vietnam-focused products across different asset classes:

  • Equity

    • Forum One - VinaCapital Vietnam Fund (VVF) is an actively managed UCITS fund that invests in public equities of companies that are based in Vietnam, with an objective to provide long-term sustainable capital appreciation, through bottom-up stock picking and disciplined risk management.

    • VinaCapital Equity Opportunity Fund (VINACAPITAL-VEOF) is an open-ended equity fund professionally managed by VinaCapital. VINACAPITAL-VEOF invests in the stocks of companies with large market capitalizations and high growth potential.

    • VinaCapital Equity Special Access Fund (VINACAPITAL-VESAF) is an open-ended equity fund professionally managed by VinaCapital. VINACAPITAL-VESAF invests in the stocks of companies with small and medium market caps and high growth potential, especially stocks that have attracted special attention from foreign investors with limited room for foreign investments.

    • VinaCapital Vietnam Access Fund (VAF) is a Cayman Islands mutual fund and invests a majority of its assets in VinaCapital Equity Special Access Fund (VINACAPITAL-VESAF), a Vietnam domiciled open-ended fund. VAF’s unique structure as a feeder fund into VINACAPITAL-VESAF enables foreign investors to be treated like local investors.

  • Fixed Income

    • VinaCapital Enhanced Fixed Income Fund (VINACAPITAL-VFF) is an open-ended bond fund professionally managed by VinaCapital. VINACAPITAL-VFF invests into corporate bonds issued by highly credit-worthy institutions and government bonds, with the main investment objective of generating higher return than 12-month deposit rate in medium-to-long term.

    • VinaCapital Liquidity Bond Fund (VINACAPITAL-VLBF) is an open-ended bond fund professionally managed by VinaCapital with a focus on principal preservation. VINACAPITAL-VLBF mainly invests in short-term fixed income securities and money market instruments to generate very stable returns while maintaining daily liquidity.

  • Balanced
    VinaCapital Insights Balanced Fund (VINACAPITAL-VIBF) is a balanced open-ended fund professionally managed by VinaCapital. VINACAPITAL-VIBF invests equally in bonds and equities and gives investors the opportunity to participate in equity upside while limiting overall portfolio volatility.

  • Exchanged Traded Fund (ETF)
    VinaCapital VN100 ETF (FUEVN100) is an Exchange Traded Fund that seeks to replicate the performance of the VN100 index, a free-float adjusted market capitalization weighted index which measures the performance of the top 100 largest and most liquid stocks listed on the Ho Chi Minh Stock Exchange (HOSE).

  • Multi Strategies
    VinaCapital Vietnam Opportunity Fund (VOF) invests growth equity in privately negotiated opportunities across listed, private equity and SOE assets. VOF is a long-term, active investor who takes significant minority stakes in leading private companies in Vietnam.

We offer Asia alternative products for investment in sectors such as Real Estate, Infrastructure, Venture Capital and Renewable Energies.

VCC: The Game Changer

The Singapore Variable Capital Company (VCC) legislation represents the city-state’s latest investment fund innovation. Adding to the existing suite of legal entities that may be used as investment vehicles, the VCC can be used for all types of investment strategies. Some of the pivotal incentives of the structure include the Grant scheme by MAS to co-fund 30% of the qualifying fund setup expenses up to S$30,000 as well as the Tax Incentives scheme under sections 13O (referred to as the “Singapore Resident Fund Scheme”) and 13U (“Enhanced-Tier Fund Scheme”) of the Singapore Income Tax Act.
